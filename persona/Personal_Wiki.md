## Summary
A concise profile of the operator behind this workspace: a detail-oriented builder focused on reliable medical-AI data operations, clear experiment bookkeeping, and fast corrective loops.

## Profile
- Works across dataset curation, labeling quality checks, and model-result pipelines.
- Values reproducibility and traceability over convenience.
- Uses direct, execution-first collaboration with explicit verification steps.

## Operating Principles
- Verify critical fields with row-level comparisons.
- Treat unexplained blanks and mismatches as issues to resolve, not ignore.
- Preserve source-of-truth lineage when generating derived files.
- Prefer concise outputs with concrete evidence (IDs, counts, paths).

## Typical Workflow
1. Request a transformation or status pull.
2. Require exact-match validation.
3. Identify mismatches and classify error type.
4. Patch source or transformation logic.
5. Rebuild artifact and confirm zero mismatches.

- Compute-job queue monitoring and triage.

## Collaboration Contract
- Keep communication concise.
- Surface assumptions and edge cases early.
- Provide file-level outputs and concrete verification metrics.
- Prefer done-and-checked deliverables over proposals.
