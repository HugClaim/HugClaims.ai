hug claims enterprise grant

Get a three-month period of free access to Hug Claims data.

The Enterprise Grant gives qualified enterprise teams and nonprofit researchers zero-cost access to Hug Claims data for up to three months. Use this period to explore, prototype, and validate what matters most for your goals, and our team can support you along the way.

What the grant includes

Data Access

Up to three months of free access to Hug Claims data, with onboarding support and integration guidance based on your selected focus areas.

Technical Enablement

A setup session for taxonomy mapping, data workflows, and reporting expectations so your team can evaluate value quickly.

Decision Package

When you are ready, you receive a recommended annual or quarterly plan aligned to your scope, governance, and budget.

Grant Application

Use your company email. Most applications receive an initial response within 3 business days.
